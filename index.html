Passage to an

Illuminated Lifestyle

M Grand Minori is meticulously designed with pillars of Japanese-inspired elegance, where every space and experience is imbued with a signature Zen opulence.

Grand Arrival

Iki-Gen

Derived from genkan (玄関), meaning entryway — emphasising a grand arrival experience, setting the tone for prestige from entrance to lobby.

Tranquil Flow

Iki-Gawa

Inspired by gawa (川), meaning water — introducing a tranquil corridor experience, seamlessly blending serenity into a serviced apartment setting.

Garden Soul

Iki-Niwa

Rooted in niwa (庭), meaning garden — infusing the facilities deck and common areas with the sophistication of exquisite Japanese garden design.

Natural Light

Iki-Mado

Drawing from mado (窓), meaning window — prioritising expansive window designs, inviting natural light to foster a luminous and airy living environment.

When does the RTS Link open?+
The RTS Link connecting Bukit Chagar (JB) to Woodlands North (Singapore) is scheduled to open on 31 December 2026. M Grand Minori is 3km (~5 min drive) from Bukit Chagar Station, with a dedicated shuttle service planned.
Can Singaporeans buy this property?+
Yes. Foreigners can purchase properties above RM1M in Johor, but M Grand Minori's pricing under RM1M is accessible for Malaysians and PR holders. Contact our team for the latest foreign buyer policies.
What's included in the RM32,000 package?+

Is this freehold?+
Yes — M Grand Minori is fully freehold. Permanent ownership with no lease renewal required, providing lasting investment security.
