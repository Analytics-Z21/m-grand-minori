Illuminated Lifestyle

M Grand Minori is meticulously designed with pillars of Japanese-inspired elegance, where every space and experience is imbued with a signature Zen opulence.

Grand Arrival

Iki-Gen

Derived from genkan (玄関), meaning entryway — emphasising a grand arrival experience, setting the tone for prestige from entrance to lobby.

Tranquil Flow

Iki-Gawa

Inspired by gawa (川), meaning water — introducing a tranquil corridor experience, seamlessly blending serenity into a serviced apartment setting.

Garden Soul

Iki-Niwa

Rooted in niwa (庭), meaning garden — infusing the facilities deck and common areas with the sophistication of exquisite Japanese garden design.

Natural Light

Iki-Mado

Drawing from mado (窓), meaning window — prioritising expansive window designs, inviting natural light to foster a luminous and airy living environment.
